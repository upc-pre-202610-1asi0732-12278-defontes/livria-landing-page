Frequent Questions

Everything you need to know about Livria, in one place.

Using the App

Download the app from Google Play or the App Store, open it, and tap "Sign Up". Enter your name, email, and a password. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link inside and your account is ready to use.

Yes. Log in with your account credentials on any device. Your library, reading progress, and preferences sync automatically across all your devices.

Our algorithm analyzes your reading history, ratings, genres you browse, and community activity to suggest books tailored to your taste. The more you use Livria, the more accurate the suggestions become.

After purchasing an e-book, open it from your Library and tap the download icon. The book will be saved to your device and accessible even without an internet connection.

Go to the "Communities" tab in the app, browse groups by genre or topic, and tap "Join". You'll immediately have access to discussions, shared reading lists, and community events.

Payments

Livria uses bank transfers as its primary payment method. When you place an order, you'll see our bank account details. Transfer the amount and upload a photo of the receipt in the app. Our team verifies it and processes your order — usually within a few hours.

Payment verification typically takes between 1 and 4 business hours. If your transfer was made outside business hours, it will be reviewed the following working day. You'll receive a push notification once confirmed.

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase for physical books that haven't been shipped yet, or for e-books with verified technical issues. To request one, contact our team via the Contact Us form with your order ID and reason.

Yes. Livria does not store any banking credentials. For third-party processors like Izipay, all transactions are handled under their own encrypted, PCI-compliant infrastructure. We only store a confirmation reference for your records.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard delivery within Lima takes 2–4 business days after payment confirmation. Orders to other regions in Peru may take 5–8 business days. Express options may be available depending on your area.

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via push notification and email. You can also check the status in real time from the "My Orders" section inside the app.

Yes. During checkout you can select "Pick-up at a local point" and choose the nearest location using our integrated map. This option is free of charge and usually available within 1–2 business days.

If your physical book arrives damaged, take a photo of the packaging and the damage and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or a full refund at no extra cost to you.

Subscriptions

A Livria subscription gives you unlimited access to our e-book catalog, exclusive discounts on physical books, early access to new releases, and priority customer support. Premium members also enjoy an ad-free experience and advanced reading analytics.

Subscriptions are renewed monthly. You authorize a monthly bank transfer for the subscription amount. You'll receive a reminder 3 days before the renewal date so you can make the transfer on time and avoid any interruption.

Yes. You can cancel anytime from "Settings → Subscription" in the app. Your benefits will remain active until the end of the current billing period, after which your account reverts to the free plan with no charges.

E-books you individually purchased remain yours permanently. Books accessed through the subscription catalog will no longer be available after cancellation, but your reading progress is saved should you decide to resubscribe.

Yes! New users get a 14-day free trial of the Premium subscription. No payment required to start — simply activate it from the Subscription section in the app. You'll be notified before the trial ends.
